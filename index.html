Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

Overview

Prism Content Planner is a personal content management tool that helps individual creators plan, review, and publish pins on Pinterest. The application includes a manual review and approval step, giving the account owner full control over every pin before it is published.

Data We Access

Prism Content Planner only accesses data explicitly authorized by the account owner through Pinterest OAuth2:

  • Pinterest account profile — to verify the connected account
  • Boards list — to let the owner select a destination board
  • Pin creation and read permissions — to publish and retrieve pins created by this app only

How We Use Data

  • Pinterest OAuth tokens are stored securely and used solely to perform actions explicitly approved by the account owner.
  • All pins go through a mandatory manual review step — nothing is published without the owner's confirmation.
  • No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.
  • No data from Pinterest users other than the account owner is accessed or stored.

Data Retention

OAuth tokens are stored in encrypted form and can be revoked at any time via Pinterest account settings or within the application. Revocation immediately terminates all API access.

Third-Party Services

Prism Content Planner integrates with the Pinterest API in accordance with the Pinterest Developer Guidelines and Pinterest Terms of Service.
